📦 Orders & Refunds

Everything you need to know about your purchases and returns.

1. Ordering Process

Orders can be placed online through our webshop. After you finalize your order, you’ll receive an email confirmation with your order details.

2. Shipping

  • 📍 We ship across Hungary and selected EU countries.
  • 🚚 Orders are usually dispatched within 2–3 business days.
  • 💸 Shipping cost will be displayed before checkout.

3. Order Status

You can track your order status from your profile under "My Orders". Email updates are sent after each status change.

4. Returns & Right of Withdrawal

According to Hungarian and EU laws, you may return any product within 14 days of receiving it – no explanation needed.

  • 📦 Product must be unused and in original packaging.
  • 📩 Contact us before sending anything back.

5. Refund Process

After receiving and inspecting the returned product, we’ll process your refund within 5–10 working days using the original payment method.

6. Exceptions

Custom-made items or gift cards are non-refundable unless faulty.
